Accessibility Statement

QuizBrawl is committed to ensuring accessibility of its website for people with disabilities. We strive to maintain compliance with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 AA, which provide standards for making web content accessible to a wide range of people with disabilities, including blindness and low vision, deafness and hearing loss, learning disabilities, cognitive limitations, limited movement, speech disabilities, photosensitivity, and combinations of these.

Accessibility Features

Our efforts to maintain accessibility include:

  • Alternative Text: Images on our website are accompanied by alternative text attributes, which aid users who listen to the content of the site by using screen readers.
  • Keyboard Navigation: Our website can be navigated using a keyboard by pressing the "Tab" key to move forward and "Shift + Tab" to move backward through links and form controls.
  • Contrast and Color: We have ensured that the colors and contrasts used on our website are accessible, making it easier for users with visual impairments to read content.
  • Readable Fonts: We use fonts that are easy to read and adjust in size without loss of content or functionality.
  • Responsive Design: Our website is designed to be responsive and compatible with different devices, ensuring a seamless experience for all users.

Feedback

We welcome feedback on the accessibility of our website. If you encounter any accessibility barriers or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us at contact@quizbrawl.com. We will do our best to address your concerns and make the necessary adjustments to improve accessibility.

Continuous Improvement

QuizBrawl is committed to providing an accessible website experience for all users. We will continue to review and enhance our website to ensure accessibility and usability for everyone.
